About the site

This site's sole purpose is to show some of my photography and possibly inspire others to get outside and do the same.

Why

Going on into the world and being able to take single moments in time and hold them forever is powerful.

Where

All of the photos on this site where taken over the state of Connecticut

How

Going out and taking photographs is surprisingly simple and accessible. With just a camera or even a smartphone, you can capture the beauty of your surroundings, whether it’s a bustling street, a quiet park, or a stunning sunset. Photography doesn’t require complicated equipment or special skills to start; all it takes is curiosity, an eye for detail, and the willingness to explore. Every walk outside is an opportunity to find interesting angles, textures, and moments that tell a story through your lens./p>
